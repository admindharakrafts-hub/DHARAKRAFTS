Product Description

Extremely loved design when it comes to a marble pot, makrana white marble is the first choice of the buyer as well as the artisan when a masterpiece like this is to be created, this shiny white marble has been artistically crafted into a beautiful vase. The intricate workmanship is reflected with the traditional colors, gold paint, and beautiful kundans embellished on it. The flower vase represents traditional Indian creativity. Offering beautifully carved Marble Handicrafts at competitive prices.

We are the manufacturers and global suppliers of the marble handicrafts, we export to USA, UK, Europe, Australia, Russia, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Sweden, Nederland, Turkey, Poland, Albania, Romania, Union, Hong Kong, Belgium, South Korea, Canada, Mexico, Singapore, Switzerland, Laos, Maldives, Bermuda, Guyana, and many other European countries.
