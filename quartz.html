Premium Engineered Quartz

Dharakrafts International is a leading supplier and exporter of premium engineered quartz stone from India. Our quartz collection features stunning Calacatta, Carrara and Crystal White varieties — each offering the beauty of natural marble with superior durability and zero maintenance. Quartz is non-porous, scratch-resistant, stain-proof and ideal for countertops, flooring, backsplashes, fireplaces and commercial applications.
