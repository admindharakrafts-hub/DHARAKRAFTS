Engineered Calacatta Quartz

Premium Engineered Quartz Manufacturer & Exporter

Dharakrafts International offers a stunning range of premium engineered quartz surfaces. Our quartz collection features marble-inspired designs — from Calacatta and Carrara patterns to pure white and custom colour options. Engineered quartz combines the beauty of natural marble with superior scratch resistance, stain resistance, and zero maintenance.

Our quartz surfaces are manufactured using advanced Breton technology, combining 93% natural quartz crystals with premium resins and pigments. The result is a non-porous, hygienic surface that is perfect for kitchen countertops, bathroom vanities, bar tops, commercial surfaces, and wall cladding.

All Popular Quartz Designs Under One Roof

We provide an extensive range of marble-look quartz designs including Calacatta Statuario, Calacatta Classic, Calacatta Noble, Calacatta Venice, Calacatta Ocean, Calacatta Sky, Carrara White, Carrara Bianco, Carrara Cloud, Carrara Alaska, Crystal White, Borghini White, Calcutta Raphael, and many more. Each design is available in jumbo slab format.

Why Import Engineered Quartz From Us?

Engineered quartz offers significant advantages over natural marble — it is non-porous, scratch-resistant, stain-resistant, and requires zero maintenance. Below are reasons to choose Dharakrafts for quartz.

  • Wide range of Calacatta, Carrara, and custom marble-look designs
  • Non-porous surface — stain, scratch, and heat resistant
  • Hygienic — bacteria and mould resistant for food preparation areas
  • Consistent colour and pattern across all slabs
  • Zero maintenance — no sealing or special cleaning required
  • Competitive factory-direct pricing for bulk orders
  • Reliable global shipping with careful slab packaging
  • Free samples available for design verification
