Product Description

With the help of contemporary equipment, Viscon White Granite is precisely cut, finished, and polished under the guidance of experienced professionals. The Viscon White Granite provided is sure to add an impressive appearance in the environment for cladding, flooring, and countertop design applications.

Main Feature of Viscon White Granite: Outstanding polish, Quick to clean, Resistance to Breakage, Ensures long-lasting gloss.

This durable and beautiful Indian Granite is suitable for indoor use in climates with freezing temperatures and for all commercial and residential projects, especially for indoor flooring, floor, and wall, backsplashes, countertops, fireplaces, surrounding tubs, and showers, etc.

Viscon White Granite is now incredibly demanding Indian granite. It's a finely-grained, sterling, and fog grey swirls and elegant granite on a white base.
