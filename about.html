Who We Are

Dharakrafts International is a government-recognized natural stone manufacturer and exporter based in the historic city of Jaipur, Rajasthan. With decades of experience in the Indian stone industry, we offer an extensive catalogue of over 100 premium stone varieties — including granite, marble, sandstone, limestone, slate, quartzite, monuments, and hand-carved statues.


Our products are exported to clients across the USA, UK, Europe, Middle East, Australia, South Africa and beyond. We pride ourselves on direct quarry sourcing, rigorous quality control, and our ability to deliver bespoke stone solutions on time, every time.
