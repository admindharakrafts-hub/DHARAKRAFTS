Who We Are

Dharakrafts International is a government-recognized manufacturer and direct exporter of premium Indian natural stone, headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan — the epicentre of the world's most prolific natural stone belt. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Mohit Lamba, the company has evolved into a vertically integrated export powerhouse, delivering precision-processed stone to architects, contractors, landscapers, and distributors across 30+ countries on five continents.

With corporate offices in Jaipur and a fully owned, state-of-the-art processing facility at SDP Industrial Area, Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu, we command the entire value chain — from quarry-face block selection and gang-saw cutting to calibration, finishing, multi-point quality inspection, export-grade packaging, and full container loading. This end-to-end control is what allows us to guarantee dimensional accuracy, surface consistency, and on-time delivery on every single order.

We are not a trading house. We are a manufacturer. Every slab, tile, monument, and sculpture that carries the Dharakrafts name is processed in our own factory, inspected by our own QC team, and packed in our own packaging unit — giving our global clients the transparency, reliability, and pricing advantage that only a direct-source manufacturer can offer.

Our Story

Dharakrafts International was founded on a conviction that the extraordinary natural stones of India — renowned for centuries and used in landmarks from the Taj Mahal to European heritage facades — deserve to reach the global stage with the precision, consistency, and professionalism that today's international buyers demand. What began as a focused stone sourcing operation in Rajasthan has grown, under Mr. Mohit Lamba's leadership, into a full-scale manufacturing and export enterprise serving clients across five continents.

Rajasthan sits on one of the world's richest geological belts for natural stone — producing the legendary Makrana White Marble, the warm-toned Fossil Mint and Raj Green Sandstones, and an unrivalled spectrum of slates, limestones, and pebbles. Our headquarters in Jaipur places us at the nerve centre of this region, giving us first-access relationships with quarry owners, master craftsmen, and multi-generation stone artisans.

Our fully owned processing factory in Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu, complements this geographic advantage with strategic depth — situated close to India's top-grade South Indian granite quarries and within easy reach of the ports of Chennai and Tuticorin. This dual-location model means faster turnaround, lower logistics costs, and the ability to fulfil complex multi-stone orders from a single, reliable source.

Our Mission

To be the world's most reliable source of premium Indian natural stone — delivering export-grade granite, marble, sandstone, slate, quartz, pebbles, monuments, and sculptures at factory-direct pricing with uncompromising quality, ethical sourcing, and on-time shipping. We exist to simplify international stone procurement for architects, builders, landscapers, and memorial companies who refuse to compromise on quality.

Our Vision

To build Dharakrafts International into the most trusted and preferred Indian natural stone brand worldwide — recognized for manufacturing excellence, product innovation, and a relentless commitment to long-term partnerships. We envision every project that demands premium Indian stone turning to Dharakrafts first — not because we are the biggest, but because we are the most reliable.

Why Global Buyers Choose Dharakrafts

Every piece passes a rigorous 6-stage quality control process — from raw block grading at the quarry to final surface, dimension, and shade verification before packaging. We don't ship anything we wouldn't approve ourselves.

Exporting to 30+ countries across Europe, North America, the Middle East, Australia, and Africa with full export documentation, LCL/FCL containerisation, and end-to-end freight coordination.

Full Customisation

Custom dimensions, thickness options, surface finishes (polished, honed, flamed, brushed, tumbled, shotblast), edge profiles, and bespoke monument designs — tailored to your exact project specification.

Own Manufacturing

Proven Reliability

Government-recognized export house with CAPEXIL membership, 500+ global clients served, and a track record of repeat orders that speaks louder than any brochure.

On-Time, Every Time
