About Us

Home / About Us

Who We Are

Dharakrafts International is a government-recognized natural stone manufacturer and exporter headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan — the heart of India's natural stone belt. Founded and led by Mr. Mohit Lamba, the company has built a reputation for delivering premium-grade Indian natural stones to clients across 30+ countries worldwide.

With our head office at B-45 Godara Colony, Jhotwara, Jaipur, and a state-of-the-art processing factory at 1C3 Kondapanayanpalli, SDP Industrial Area, Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu, we control the entire value chain — from raw block extraction at the quarry to precision cutting, finishing, quality inspection, export packaging, and container loading. This vertical integration allows us to guarantee consistent quality, competitive pricing, and on-time delivery for every order.

Our Story

Dharakrafts International was born from a simple belief: that the extraordinary natural stones of India deserve to be showcased to the world with the quality, reliability, and professionalism that international buyers expect. Under the leadership of Mr. Mohit Lamba, we have grown from a regional stone supplier into a trusted global export house serving clients on every continent.

Rajasthan is home to some of the world's finest quarries — producing everything from the iconic Makrana White Marble that built the Taj Mahal to the richly textured sandstones and slates used in heritage buildings across Europe. Our deep roots in this region give us unmatched access to raw materials, skilled artisans, and generations of stone-working knowledge.

Our factory in Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu, adds strategic depth — positioned close to South Indian granite quarries and major export ports like Chennai and Tuticorin, enabling faster turnaround times for granite, monument, and statue orders destined for international markets.

Our Mission

To supply premium quality Indian natural stones at the most competitive prices to our global customers, while maintaining the highest standards of ethical sourcing, fair labour practices, and environmental responsibility. We aim to be a one-stop partner for architects, builders, and landscapers who demand excellence.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in Indian natural stone exports — recognised worldwide for uncompromising quality, innovative product development, and a commitment to building long-term relationships with every client we serve. We envision a future where Dharakrafts stone is the first choice for premium projects across the globe.
