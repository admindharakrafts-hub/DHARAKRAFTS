Premium Indian Granite

Indian granite is renowned worldwide for its exceptional durability, timeless beauty and remarkable variety. At Dharakrafts International, we offer a wide range of premium granite varieties sourced directly from quarries across India — from dramatic blacks and rich reds to exotic blues, pristine whites and multi-colour exotics. Our granite is available in polished, honed, flamed, brushed and natural finishes, in tiles, slabs and custom-cut formats.
