A Leading Indian Granite Manufacturer & Exporter

Dharakrafts International is recognized as one of India's most trusted granite manufacturers and exporters. India is renowned globally for its exceptional granite varieties — from the prestigious Absolute Black and Pearl White to exotic Blue Dunes and Paradisso Bash. We source premium granite directly from the finest quarries across South India, Rajasthan, and Karnataka.

Our advanced factory in Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu, features state-of-the-art gang-saw cutting, multi-disc cutting, polishing, honing, flaming, and leathering capabilities. This enables us to produce granite slabs, tiles, countertops, and custom-cut products to the highest international standards.

We export premium Indian granite to the UK, USA, Europe, Middle East, and Australia — serving architects, builders, and stone importers worldwide with consistent quality, competitive pricing, and reliable delivery.

All Popular Indian Granite Variants Under One Roof

We provide all popular Indian granite varieties like Absolute Black, P White, Viscon White, Blue Dunes, Golden Beach, Kerala Green, Classic Paradise, Paradisso Bash, Rajasthan Black, Grey Flamed, and others. We offer a wide range of granite products like slabs, tiles, countertops, vanity tops, steps and risers, wall cladding, cobbles, and custom-cut products.

The popular granite finishes we provide are polished, honed, flamed, leather, bush hammered, and sandblasted. At Dharakrafts International, we strive to deliver world-class granite products that exceed our buyers' expectations.

Why Import Indian Granite From Us?

Indian granite is one of the hardest and most durable natural stones available. It has gained immense popularity in international markets due to its consistent quality, colour variety, and competitive pricing. Below are some major reasons why you should choose Dharakrafts as your preferred granite import partner.

  • Direct access to India's finest granite quarries for premium block selection
  • In-house gang-saw and multi-disc cutting for precise slab production
  • Full range of finishes: Polished, Honed, Flamed, Leather, Bush Hammered
  • Strict quality control for colour consistency, thickness, and surface finish
  • Competitive pricing due to direct quarry-to-factory-to-port supply chain
  • Expert packing in wooden crates for safe, sea-worthy export
  • Insurance coverage on all consignments
  • Dedicated project support from sampling to delivery

Available Granite Product Sizes

Our granite processing capabilities allow us to produce a comprehensive range of sizes and formats. Some of the commonly available granite product sizes are:

Slabs: 180 x 60 CM and above (up to 300 x 200 CM gang-sawn)

Tiles: 30 x 30, 60 x 30, 60 x 60 CM in 10-20mm thickness

Countertop Slabs: 250 x 65 CM, 300 x 180 CM (2 CM and 3 CM thick)

Vanity Tops: Custom sizes with sink cutouts

Steps & Risers: 120 x 30 CM, 150 x 30 CM in 2-3 CM thickness

Wall Cladding: 60 x 15, 60 x 30 CM in 1-2 CM thickness

Cobbles: 10 x 10 CM in various thicknesses

Edges: Machine Cut, Bevelled, Bullnosed, Ogee

Please note that we also provide custom-sized granite products as per the exact requirements of our buyers.

Industry-leading Processing Capabilities

We use cutting-edge tools and industry-leading facilities at our state-of-the-art granite processing plant. Our factory is equipped with gang-saws, multi-disc cutters, edge-cutting machines, polishing lines, honing machines, flaming equipment, and leathering tools — enabling us to produce premium granite products with a high degree of precision.

Delivering Quality Granite Products

Delivering high-quality granite to our customers is our constant goal at Dharakrafts International. We pay attention to every step, from block selection at the quarry to the consignment's final delivery, ensuring our customers receive exactly what they requested.

We consider every aspect of granite quality including surface finish, colour uniformity, slab thickness, edge quality, and stain resistance. Our team of skilled experts ensures that our quality control procedures are successful at every stage of production.
