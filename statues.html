Exquisite Marble Statues & Handicrafts From India

Dharakrafts International is a premier manufacturer and exporter of hand-carved marble statues and artisan handicrafts from India. Our collection features exquisite religious statues, decorative sculptures, and handcrafted marble accessories — all created by master artisans of Rajasthan using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

From magnificent Lord Buddha and Ganesh statues to elegant Lady statues and decorative marble lamps, each piece is hand-carved from premium white Makrana marble and meticulously finished with polishing and optional painted details. Our statues and handicrafts are exported to temples, luxury hotels, galleries, and private collectors worldwide.

We take pride in preserving India's rich sculptural heritage while meeting the demands of modern interior design and architectural décor. Every piece from Dharakrafts is a unique work of art that carries the soul of Indian craftsmanship.

Our Statue & Handicraft Collection

We provide an extensive range of hand-carved marble statues and handicrafts including Lord Ganesh, Lord Shiva, Lord Buddha, Maa Durga, Hanuman Ji, Saraswati, Radha Krishna, Ram Darbar, Kali Maa, Mahatma Gandhi, Subhash Chandra Bose, Elephant, Lion, Lady Statues, and various handcrafted items like marble lamps, vases, pots, and wine glass pairs.

Why Choose Dharakrafts for Marble Statues?

Indian marble statues are world-famous for their exquisite craftsmanship and attention to detail. Here's why buyers worldwide trust Dharakrafts.

  • Master artisans with decades of traditional marble carving experience
  • Premium Makrana marble — the same stone used to build the Taj Mahal
  • Exquisite hand-carved detailing on every piece
  • Full customization: size, pose, finish, and painted accents
  • Careful wooden crate packaging with foam protection for safe transit
  • Competitive factory-direct pricing for wholesale and retail
  • Reliable global shipping with insurance coverage
  • Custom sculpting available for portraits and bespoke designs
