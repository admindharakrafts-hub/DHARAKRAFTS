Hand-Carved Stone Statues & Handicraft

Dharakrafts International offers an exquisite range of hand-carved marble and granite statues, as well as premium marble handicrafts — from religious deities and decorative figures to elegant marble lamps, vases and wine glasses. Our master artisans in Rajasthan combine traditional carving techniques with modern precision to deliver museum-quality pieces in any size or design.
