World-Class Manufacturing Facility

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu span thousands of square feet, equipped with the latest stone-cutting and processing machinery. From raw block intake to precision-finished slabs and monuments, every stage of production happens under our roof — giving us complete control over quality and lead times.

The factory houses multiple automatic gang-saw cutting lines, surface polishing lines, CNC profiling machines, and an overhead crane system for safe handling of heavy stone blocks. Our skilled workforce operates around the clock to fulfil orders for clients across 30+ countries.
