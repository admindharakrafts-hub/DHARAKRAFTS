Product Description

Absolute Black Granite is durable and beautiful Indian Granite that is suitable for indoor/outdoor use in climates with freezing temperatures and for all commercial and residential projects, especially for indoor flooring, floor & wall, backsplashes, countertops, fireplaces, surrounding tubs & showers, etc.

Presently, Absolute Black Granite is a highly demanding Granite from India. Absolute Black granite is mined from near Warrangal, Andhra Pradesh, India.

We are one of India's trusted producers, manufacturers, and exporters of Absolute Black Granite alias Nero Assoluto Granite. Our Absolute Black Granite is India's legendary Indian Black Granite. At the rock bottom prices of our granite factory in India, we will supply good quality Absolute Black Granite Tiles, Absolute Black Granite Slabs, Absolute Black Granite Stairs and Windowsill, Absolute Black Granite Countertops, Absolute Black Granite Vanity Tops, etc.

Dharakrafts International is a Universal Exporter of Absolute Black Granite Slabs tiles, blocks, cobblestones, pebbles, monuments, and headstones.
