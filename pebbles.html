Natural Stone Pebbles

Dharakrafts International offers a beautiful range of natural stone pebbles for landscaping, garden decoration, interior design and aquarium use. Our pebbles come in a wide variety of colors, sizes and finishes — from polished black river pebbles to vibrant mix-color varieties. They are ideal for walkways, fountains, flower pots, vases and all manner of decorative applications.
