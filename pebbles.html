A Leading Natural Pebbles Manufacturer & Exporter From India

Dharakrafts International is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of premium natural pebbles from India. Our pebble collection includes river pebbles, polished pebbles, tumbled pebbles, and epoxy-coated decorative pebbles in a vibrant range of colours — from classic black and white to multi-colour blends and exotic tones.

Our pebbles are carefully sourced from Indian river beds and processed at our facility with washing, grading, tumbling, and polishing to ensure consistent quality and size. We supply pebbles for landscaping, garden decoration, water features, pool surrounds, interior décor, and construction applications worldwide.

All Popular Pebble Variants Under One Roof

We provide all popular natural pebble varieties including Black River Pebbles, White River Pebbles, Grey River Flat Pebbles, Mix Colour Pebbles, Natural Multi Colour Pebbles, Small Black Pebbles, Natural Beige & Red River Pebbles, and Epoxied Mix Colour Pebbles. Each variety is available in multiple size grades and finishes.

Why Import Natural Pebbles From Us?

Natural pebbles add organic beauty and texture to any landscaping or interior project. Below are some reasons to choose Dharakrafts as your pebble import partner.

  • Wide range of natural colours: Black, White, Multi, Grey, Red, Beige
  • Consistent size grading with multiple washing and sorting stages
  • Available in natural, polished, and epoxy-coated finishes
  • Bulk supply capability — from 1 ton to full container loads
  • Export-standard packaging in moisture-resistant bags
  • Competitive factory-direct pricing
  • Reliable shipping worldwide
  • Free samples available for quality assessment

Available Pebble Sizes & Packaging

Medium: 30-60mm (popular for landscaping, garden borders)

Large: 60-100mm, 100-200mm (for water features, pathways)

Shapes: Natural Rounded, Flat/Oval, Tumbled

Finishes: Natural Unpolished, Polished, Epoxy Coated
