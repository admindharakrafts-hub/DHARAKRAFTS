A Leading Sandstone & Limestone Exporter From India

Dharakrafts International is recognized as one of India's most trusted sandstone and limestone manufacturers and exporters. With deep roots in the natural stone industry, we source premium sandstone and limestone directly from the finest quarries across Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, and South India.

Our state-of-the-art factory in Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu, is equipped with advanced cutting, calibrating, and finishing machinery, enabling us to deliver precisely processed sandstone and limestone tiles, slabs, cobbles, and custom-cut products. We export to the UK, USA, Europe, Australia, and Gulf countries.

In regard to quality and environmental sustainability, we maintain the highest standards. Our quality system encompasses all activities that influence the products and services we offer and is fully integrated into our overall business management systems. Our manufacturing units are audited for safe, secure, and sustainable operations.

All Popular Indian Sandstone & Limestone Variants Under One Roof

We provide all popular Indian sandstone and limestone varieties like Kandla Grey, Mint, Camel Dust, Raj Green, Mandana Red, Fossil Mint, Lalitpur Yellow, Kota Blue, Kota Brown, Tandur Grey, Tandur Yellow, Kadappa Black, Dholpur Beige, and others. We offer a wide range of products like tiles, cutter slabs, wall cladding, mosaic, pool coping, cobbles, crazy paving, circles, stepping stones, and more.

The popular sandstone and limestone finishes we provide are natural, brushed, tumbled, tumbled + brushed, honed, sandblasted, and calibrated.
