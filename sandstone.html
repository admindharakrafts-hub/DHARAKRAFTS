Premium Indian Sandstone & Limestone

India is home to some of the world's finest sandstone and limestone quarries. Dharakrafts International offers a comprehensive range of premium sandstone and limestone varieties — from the iconic Kandla Grey and Mint Sandstone to the durable Kota Brown Limestone. Our stones are ideal for exterior paving, wall cladding, flooring, landscaping and architectural applications. Available in natural, honed, brushed, tumbled and calibrated finishes.
