A Leading Indian Marble Manufacturer & Exporter

Dharakrafts International is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of premium Indian marble. India is home to some of the world's finest marble quarries — from the legendary Makrana White (used to build the Taj Mahal) to exotic varieties like Fantasy Brown, Lavender Fusion, and Indian Statuario.

Our factory features advanced marble processing equipment including gang-saw cutting, edge polishing, surface honing, and quality inspection systems. This enables us to produce perfectly sized marble slabs, tiles, and custom-cut products for luxury residential and commercial projects worldwide.

We export Indian marble to the UK, USA, Europe, Middle East, and Asia — serving architects, interior designers, and marble importers with consistent quality and competitive pricing.

All Popular Indian Marble Variants Under One Roof

We provide all popular Indian marble varieties like Makrana White, Morwad White, Indian Statuario, Carrera Polish, Diana Italian, Fantasy Brown, Lavender Fusion, Indo Italian, Indo Pista, Indian Green, Nano White, Vietnam White, and others. We offer a wide range of marble products like slabs, tiles, countertops, vanity tops, steps and risers, wall cladding, and custom-cut products.

The popular marble finishes we provide are polished and honed. At Dharakrafts International, we are committed to offering the finest quality marble at competitive rates to buyers worldwide.

Why Import Indian Marble From Us?

Indian marble is renowned for its timeless beauty, elegant veining, and exceptional durability. Below are some major reasons why you should choose Dharakrafts as your preferred marble import partner.

  • Access to India's most prestigious marble quarries including Makrana
  • Expert slab selection for colour and vein pattern consistency
  • Advanced polishing and honing for mirror-perfect finishes
  • Strict quality grading to minimize colour variation across orders
  • Careful wooden crate packing with foam protection for safe transit
  • Competitive factory-direct pricing
  • Reliable global shipping to UK, USA, Europe, and Middle East
  • Dedicated marble specialist for project consultations

Available Marble Product Sizes

Our marble processing capabilities allow us to produce a wide range of sizes. Some of the commonly available marble product sizes are:

Slabs: 180 x 60 CM and above (up to 300 x 180 CM)

Tiles: 30 x 30, 60 x 30, 60 x 60 CM in 15-20mm thickness

Countertop Slabs: Custom sizes in 2 CM and 3 CM thickness

Vanity Tops: Custom sizes with sink cutouts

Steps & Risers: 120 x 30, 150 x 30 CM

Wall Cladding: Various sizes in 10-15mm thickness

Edges: Machine Cut, Bevelled, Bullnosed, Ogee

Custom sizes and special finishes are available on request. Contact us with your project specifications.

Delivering Quality Marble Products

Delivering high-quality marble to our customers is our constant goal. We pay attention to every step, from block selection at the quarry to the final delivery. We consider every aspect of marble quality including surface finish, colour uniformity, veining consistency, slab thickness, and edge quality to guarantee flawless products.
