Our CSR Commitments

No Child Labour

  • Full compliance with Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu child labour laws.
  • Strict minimum age policy of 18 years for all factory and quarry roles.
  • Explicit prohibition of child labour enforced across all supplier and sub-contractor relationships.
  • Regular audits to verify compliance at every level of our supply chain.

No Forced Labour

  • Every employee retains the absolute right to terminate employment without penalty.
  • No deposit or document retention schemes are used in our hiring process.
  • Transparent employment contracts provided in the worker's native language.
  • No bonded labour or coercive working conditions are tolerated under any circumstance.

Health & Safety

  • Provision of PPE (masks, gloves, goggles, safety boots) to all factory staff at no cost.
  • Clean filtered drinking water and sanitary facilities available at all sites.
  • Annual health check-ups provided for all permanent employees.
  • Emergency first-aid systems and trained safety personnel on-site at all times.
  • Silica dust exposure controls strictly enforced at all cutting and polishing units.

Freedom of Association

  • Employees are free to associate, organise, and collectively bargain without interference.
  • Worker representatives participate in management discussions on workplace matters.
  • Clear grievance redressal mechanisms available to every employee.
  • No retaliation against workers who raise concerns in good faith.

Non-Discrimination

  • Equal employment opportunity regardless of race, caste, religion, gender, or disability.
  • Zero tolerance for workplace harassment, bullying, or discriminatory conduct.
  • Hiring and promotion decisions are based solely on merit and qualifications.
  • Women constitute a significant portion of our skilled workforce in finishing units.

Disciplinary Practices

  • All disciplinary procedures adhere to the Industrial Disputes Act of India.
  • Absolute prohibition of corporal punishment, verbal abuse, and harsh treatment.
  • Documented disciplinary process with right of reply for all employees.
  • Management trained in conflict resolution and constructive feedback methods.

Reasonable Working Hours

  • Standard working week of 48 hours with defined lunch and rest breaks.
  • Overtime is voluntary, compensated at premium rates, and capped at legal limits.
  • Annual earned leave, casual leave, and paid public holidays provided to all staff.
  • Night shift workers receive additional allowances and priority health screenings.

Fair Remuneration

  • All wages meet or exceed state minimum wage thresholds for skilled and unskilled labour.
  • Salary structures are transparent and communicated clearly to every employee.
  • Annual performance appraisals with merit-based increments and incentives.
  • Provident Fund (PF) and ESI contributions made on time for all eligible workers.

Management Systems

  • Dedicated senior management representatives oversee CSR and compliance programmes.
  • Health & Safety coordinators appointed at each manufacturing facility.
  • Regular internal audits and third-party inspections of working conditions.
  • CSR policies reviewed annually and updated to reflect changing regulations and best practices.

Supplier & Contractor Control

  • All quarry partners and sub-contractors are vetted against our CSR standards before engagement.
  • Supplier contracts include binding social compliance clauses.
  • Periodic on-site visits conducted at key quarry and processing partner sites.
  • Non-compliant suppliers are given corrective action timelines or are delisted.

Together, We Build Responsibly

Our CSR commitments are not just policy documents — they are the foundation of how we operate every single day. When you source from Dharakrafts, you source from a partner that cares about people and the planet as much as it cares about quality stone.
