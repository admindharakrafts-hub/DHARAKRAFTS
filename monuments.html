Custom Granite Monuments

Dharakrafts International specialises in the manufacture and export of custom granite monuments, headstones and tombstones. Our monuments are crafted from premium Indian granite and are available in a wide range of shapes, sizes and carving designs. We supply monuments to funeral homes, memorial parks and stone retailers across Europe, the USA, Australia and beyond. Custom engraving, laser etching, bronze plaques and bespoke designs are all available on request.

Premium Granite Monuments Manufacturer & Exporter

Dharakrafts International is a leading manufacturer and exporter of premium granite monuments and tombstones. Our monuments are hand-crafted from the finest Indian and imported granites — including Absolute Black, Shanxi Black, and various grey and brown granite varieties — ensuring lasting beauty and dignity for memorial installations.

Our skilled artisans and advanced stone processing facility produce monuments in a wide range of styles — from classic upright headstones and heart-shaped memorials to Celtic crosses and custom-designed family monuments. Every monument is mirror-polished and hand-finished to the highest standards before export.

All Popular Monument Styles Available

We provide a comprehensive range of granite monument styles including single headstones, family monuments, serp-top designs, oval monuments, heart-shaped memorials, Celtic cross monuments, and custom-designed memorials. All monuments are available in Absolute Black, Shanxi Black, Grey, Brown, Blue Pearl, and other granite varieties.

Why Import Granite Monuments From Us?

Indian granite is world-renowned for its exceptional hardness, colour consistency, and competitive pricing — making it ideal for memorial monuments. Here's why buyers choose Dharakrafts.

  • Premium granite selection — Absolute Black, Shanxi Black, Grey, Brown
  • Expert hand carving and mirror polishing by skilled artisans
  • Full customization: shapes, sizes, engraving, accessories
  • Strict quality inspection before every shipment
  • Careful wooden crate packaging for damage-free transit
  • Competitive factory-direct pricing for wholesalers
  • Reliable shipping to UK, USA, Europe, Australia, and worldwide
  • Sample monuments available for quality verification

Available Monument Sizes & Customization

Single Monuments: 90 x 60 x 8 CM, 90 x 70 x 8 CM, 105 x 75 x 8 CM (custom sizes)

Family Monuments: Larger sizes for multiple-name memorials

Shapes: Serp Top, Oval, Heart, Cross, Celtic Cross, Custom profiles

Materials: Absolute Black, Shanxi Black, Grey, Brown, Blue Pearl, Paradisso

Finish: Mirror Polished, Honed, Rock-pitched edges

Customization: Laser engraving, hand carving, bronze fittings, vase accessories

We offer complete customization — from monument shape and size to engraving design and accessories.
