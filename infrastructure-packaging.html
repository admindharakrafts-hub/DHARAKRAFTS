Packaging Unit

Home / Infrastructure / Packaging Unit

Export-Grade Packaging for Safe Global Delivery

The final step before your order leaves our facility is equally as important as the first. Our dedicated packaging unit ensures every stone reaches your destination — whether in the USA, UK, Europe, or Australia — in exactly the same condition it left our factory in. We use export-grade, sea-worthy packaging materials designed to withstand ocean freight conditions.

Slabs and tiles are individually wrapped in foam or bubble wrap, stacked on sturdy wooden pallets, strapped with PP bands, and loaded into numbered wooden crates or bundles. Monuments and statues receive additional foam wrapping, corner guards, and reinforced crating. Container loading is done by trained staff using forklifts to maximise safety and space efficiency.

Our Packaging Methods

Wooden Crates & Bundles

Custom-built export-grade wooden crates are used for tiles, slabs, and smaller monument pieces. Each crate is individually numbered and labelled with product details.

  • Sea-worthy, fumigated wood
  • Individual piece foam-wrapping inside crates
  • PP strap banding for security
  • Numbered and labelled for easy identification

Container Loading

Packed crates and pallets are loaded into 20ft or 40ft shipping containers by trained staff using forklifts and manual handling equipment, optimised for safe stacking and maximum load efficiency.

  • 20ft & 40ft container options
  • Forklift-assisted loading
  • Dunnage and airbag stabilisation
  • Photographic pre-shipment documentation

Outdoor Packing Yard

Our spacious outdoor packing yard accommodates large-volume orders with staged pallet storage, crate assembly, and strapping — all before container loading begins.

  • Large-volume order capacity
  • Organised lot-wise pallet staging
  • Covered storage for weather protection
  • FIFO dispatch management

Forklift & Logistics

Our yard is equipped with forklifts and pallet jacks to safely handle heavy stone crates from storage to container loading. Trained operators ensure every movement is secure.

  • Electric and diesel forklifts on-site
  • Safe handling of loads up to 5 tonnes
  • Coordination with freight forwarders
  • Full export documentation support
