A Leading Indian Slate Manufacturer & Exporter

Dharakrafts International is a leading manufacturer and exporter of premium Indian slate. India produces an exceptional variety of natural slate in colours ranging from deep black and rich green to rustic multi-colour, copper, silver-grey, and chocolate tones. Our slate is sourced from the finest mines across Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, and Andhra Pradesh.

Natural slate is a metamorphic stone renowned for its durability, weather resistance, and timeless beauty. It is ideal for wall cladding, roofing, flooring, feature walls, and landscape applications. Our factory processes slate into precisely calibrated tiles, strips, and custom formats to meet international quality standards.

All Popular Indian Slate Variants Under One Roof

We provide all popular Indian slate varieties like Deoli Green, Copper, Silver Grey, Silver Shine, Jak Black, Kund Multi, Kund Black, Multi Pink, Jeera Green, California Gold, India Autumn, Chocolate, Fossil Mint, Terra Red, Terra Yellow, Green Rustic, H White, D Green, M Green, South Black, Kodak, and others. We offer a wide range of slate products like tiles, wall cladding strips, roofing slates, crazy paving, and custom-cut formats.

The popular slate finishes we provide are natural cleft, honed, and polished. At Dharakrafts International, we are committed to offering the widest range of premium Indian slate at competitive prices.

Why Import Indian Slate From Us?

Indian slate is among the finest natural stone products available worldwide, known for its excellent weather resistance and natural anti-slip properties. Below are some reasons why you should choose Dharakrafts as your preferred slate import partner.

  • India's widest range of natural slate colours and varieties
  • Precision calibration for consistent thickness across all tiles
  • Excellent weather resistance — ideal for outdoor and roofing applications
  • Naturally anti-slip surface for safe flooring and paving
  • Competitive direct-from-factory pricing
  • Export-standard wooden crate packaging for safe transit
  • Reliable global shipping to UK, USA, Europe, Australia, and Gulf
  • Dedicated project support from sampling to delivery

Available Slate Product Sizes

Our slate processing capabilities allow us to produce a variety of sizes and formats:
